Do not continue before reading the instructions below.

  1. On the next page, you will be shown 10 pieces of text that come from a single person's public Twitter account. In bold, before each piece of text, we describe what kind of text it is. There is a short guide below.
    1. Tweet: A tweet is something that user typed and posted directly onto their public Twitter account for others to see.
    2. Retweet: A retweet is something that was originally posted by someone other than the user, but that the user wanted to share on their public Twitter account.
    3. Quote: A quote is the same as a retweet and is shared on the user's public Twitter account, but the user has the ability to add a short comment with their own perspective.
    4. Reply: A reply is a user's response directly to another Tweet. Replies are also posted on the user's public Twitter account.
  2. Please read each piece of text carefully, and when you are done, make a judgement about the user's political party affiliation at the bottom of the page, then press Submit. It is okay to say "I can't tell".
  3. You will repeat this process 5 times before seeing the "Thank you for Submitting" page. Please click the button there to finalize your submission.
  4. If you would like to participate again, you can re-click the link that you used to participate in the first place.
  5. When you are ready to begin, please enter your participant ID in the box on the right hand side of the screen. If you are a Prolific or MTurk worker, this is your worker ID. If you are a research assistant, you can just put your first name followed by the first letter of your last name. Then, press "Enter".
